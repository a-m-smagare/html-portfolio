The Best Movies according to Anna

My top 3 movies of all time

Harry Potter (all parts)

A magic movie collection that allows you to escape into a complex world of magic,
filled with wonderful orchestra music and warm, nostalgic cinematography
that send you back into childhood.

Call me by your name.

A wonderful summer time movie based in Northern Italy. It reminds you of lush summers filled
with adventures and emotions and of course love. The cinematography portrays the summer vibe
so well, it leaves you inspired to go out and look for the beauty in nature.

Dead Poets Society

Another classic. Inspires you to the core to create art and write your heart out.
The wonderful aesthetic of this movie transports you into the English school life of a few friends
who sneak out into the woods at night to read poetry to each other.
Inspired by their new professor, they get closer to themselves and start to live again, through poetry.
